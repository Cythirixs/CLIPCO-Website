Transfer Enrollment Procedures

Step One:
You have to live within the district boundaries at the time you register for school in order to apply to the program. In order to remain in the program CUSD residency is required. CLIP is one of the four alternative programs serving the Cupertino Union School District. Read through the information on this web site, research immersion education, understand the school's philosophy and make sure it's right for your child and your family.

Step Two:
Beginning in December of each year, parents interested in the CLIP program must register online at the Meyerholz Elementary School website to schedule a guided tour and presentation. In February, during the open enrollment period, only parents who have attended a guided tour and presentation can return to Meyerholz to register for CLIP and apply for the lottery.

Parents of incoming kindergarten children must also register at their neighborhood elementary school and receive a registration receipt.
