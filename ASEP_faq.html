Frequently Asked Questions About ASEP

Frequently asked Questions

Is ASEP program available for the CLIP students who don’t continue with CLIP middle school program?

This program is only offered to all Meyerholz students and CLIP middle school students. Middle school students who do not continue with CLIP will not be able to enroll for any of the ASEP classes. Exception will be allowed only if there is spaces available for any of the ASEP classes.

My kid is in 5th grade wants to be on the Meyerholz sport team. Do you know if this conflicts with Lion Dance? Can he do both? Can we sign him up if we don’t get into the Meyerholz sports team?

The Meyerholz upper grades sport team schedule conflicts with ASEP classes on Mondays and Fridays. Please also keep in mind that the sport team uses lottery system when there is more interests than the team members allowed. Futhermore, the start date of the sport team is after ASEP start date so you won’t be able to join any of the ASEP classes after the sport team lottery.

My kid is in 2nd grade and never done Wushu before. May my kid sign up for Wushu I so he can go to Lion Dance 2 and Percussion after?

No, Wushu I is reserved for Kinder only.

Why are you not offering Claymation and Cartoon Drawing this year?

We have limited space and time slots. ASEP classes vary from year to year based on space and instructors availibiilty.

My daughter has been dancing at a studio for 2 years. This past year, she has been in 2 weekly dance classes and 1 weekly chinese gymnastics class. Is she qualified for Dance II?

ASEP Chinese dance II classes requirement is based on two years of ASEP dance experience. Any extra spots on dance II will be filled from dance I list based on instructor’s discretion.

My kid is in the lower/primary grade. Given the time that they get off of school, where do they go before the ASEP classes? And is there also some place they can go after the program is over before I can come pick him up from school?

You will need to pick up your kid after school and drop him off 5 minutes before classtime, you will also need to pick him up ontime after class. The other option is to sign him up for YMCA care and the YMCA staffs will make sure he get to ASEP class and return to YMCA after class. You could also team up with other parents to setup a rotation childcare schedule before and after class.

I am an incoming kinder; where do I get registration info?

The registration info is given out during the Incoming Kinder orientation in May before school ends

My child is entering Miller Middle and I am not sure will there be any conflicts of schedule with Lion Dance. Can we decide next year once he/she sees what activities she has at Miller for after school?

ASEP needs to finalize the number of ASEP students at the end of the enrollment period so that we can secure the facility and instructors.

Additional Info regarding Miller Middle School after school activities -

Miller school days ends at 3:00pm and here is Miller 2010-2011 after school activities for your reference, there might be other activities that we are not aware of.

  1. The Miller sports are on rotation schedule every 2 months. The practices and games are on Monday through Thursday after school.
  2. The Miller clubs and organizations meet either during lunch or after school.
  3. Miller occasionally has Corral social events on Fridays (about 3-4 times a year)
  4. Miller Dance happens on Friday evenings 3-4 times a year

Can my child sit around the classroom before class start?

NO, there is no childcare before and after class. Child needed to be picked up ON time.

Can my child just hang around campus until I get off work?

NO, there is no childcare before and after class. Child needed to be picked up ON time.

Can I pick up my child on the curb side?

NO, you must pick up your child from the classroom.
