List of Programs

ASEP

After school enrichment program. This parent funded program provides classes in Chinese culture and arts to supplement the Mandarin learning experience.

Chinese New Year Parade

Every year the CLIP program participates in the San Francisco Chinese New Year Parade. Students play Chinese Cymbals and Drums and dress up in Chinese New Year costumes.

Clip Art

CLIP Art is a language and cultural enrichment class focused on introducing chinese art that connects with the CLIP curriculum.
