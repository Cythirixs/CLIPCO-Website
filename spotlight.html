Students

Mina Chao

Q:Who are you?

Mina: My name is Mina Chao (趙立鳴) and I am in 7th grade.

Q:How long have you been in the CLIP program?

Mina: I have been in CLIP for 8 years (including this year).

Q:What are some of your hobbies or special interests?

Mina: Some of my hobbies include volleyball, art, and playing cards with my friends.

Q:What's your favorite after-school activity?

Mina: My favorite after school activities include after-school sports at Miller. I was part of the volleyball, softball, and track and field teams.
The coaches are super-supportive and always do their best to help you become awesome at your sport.

Q: Tell us about one special activity from CLIP that you enjoyed.

One special activity from CLIP that I enjoy is participating in the San Francisco Chinese New Year parade. I've done it since I was
in kindergarten and I love it so much. Not only do we get to go to San Francisco and perform for thousands of people, we get to learn
cool dance moves and other skills. Sometimes, if we get lucky, we get to be broadcast on television! All the instructors are very
nice and encouraging, and make the experience way more fun.

Q: What do you love about CLIP?

One reason that I love CLIP is that you get to do a lot of special things, like learn more about Chinese culture in CLIP Art and
have long Chinese New Year celebrations. Also, if you're in CLIP you get to participate in cool ASEP classes like baking, which is super
fun because you get to bake all sorts of yummy treats for yourself and your family.
