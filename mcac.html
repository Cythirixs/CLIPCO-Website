What is MCAC?

MCAC is the Meyerholz CLIP Advisory Committee. We are a group of parents, teachers, and administrators who, together, we evaluate current and potential new curriculum and programs, fine tune the CLIP budget for CLIPCO's approval, and continuously evaluate the CLIP program of effectiveness. We are the group that takes parent's feedback about the program and brings it to the teachers and administration. MCAC is open to all parents and looks to have parent representation for all grades at the elementary level.
